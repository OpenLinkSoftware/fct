Results Ranking

Results are sorted on a combination of text match score and graph link structure. The text score is proportional to the frequency of the search terms in the match and if many terms are present, the score is increased if the different terms occur in close proximity.

Additionally, all URIs have a score based on how frequently they are referenced in properties of other URIs. The score is increased in function of the score of the referencing URI. This is similar to result ranking for web search engines. When a list of URI's is shown, for example when they occur as property values, they are sorted by the URI's score since there is no text criterion on them. The details of how scores vary in function of referencing properties, how distance affects score etc will vary as we test different options.
